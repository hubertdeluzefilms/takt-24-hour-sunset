THE FILM

24 HOUR SUNSET gives us access to the thoughts, inspirations and practices of legendary artists, world famous art dealers, appraised curators and collectors, as well as the young upcoming artists.

COME AND SPEND 24 HOURS IN THE ARTISTIC LIFE OF L.A.

From a ride on Sunset Boulevard in a convertible car at the sunrise, going through a lunch with the art dealer Patrick Painter and a visit to Peter Shire’s studio… having a beer and a deep talk with Paul McCarthy… playing basketball with two young upcoming artists in Venice… calling Raymond Pettibon stucked in New-York…
